# Getting started

New to Yeliztli? This is the short path from zero to exploring your own genome:

1. **[Install it](install-quickstart.md)** — get Yeliztli running on your machine.
2. **[Upload your DNA](upload-your-dna.md)** — import your 23andMe or AncestryDNA raw data.
3. **[Read your results](reading-your-results.md)** — understand the dashboard, findings, and
   evidence ratings.

!!! danger "Before you start"
    Yeliztli is for **research and educational use only** and is **not** a medical test.
    Please read **[Intended use & disclaimers](../intended-use.md)** — it explains, with
    evidence, why consumer DNA data must not be used for medical decisions without clinical
    confirmation.

Want more depth on installation, configuration, or self-hosting? See the
**[Install & self-host](../install/index.md)** section. Curious what each analysis covers?
See the **[module reference](../modules/index.md)**.
